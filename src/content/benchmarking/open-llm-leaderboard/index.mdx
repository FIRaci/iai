## Tổng quan

<Note type="info">
**Open LLM Leaderboard** từ Hugging Face là comprehensive benchmark cho comparing LLMs. Theo dõi performance của open models.
</Note>

## Sử dụng

1. Truy cập [hugging.co/spaces/HuggingFaceH4/open_llm_leaderboard](https://huggingface.co/spaces/HuggingFaceH4/open_llm_leaderboard)
2. Compare models by benchmark scores
3. Filter by size, architecture, v.v.
